Quick Answer

65,000 Energy = 1.5 TRX. A typical TRC-20 USDT transfer uses about ~65,000 Energy, so the energy fee is 1.5 TRX. Adding a small bandwidth burn (~0.1 TRX), total per transfer is roughly ~1.6 TRX.

Energy & Bandwidth Cheatsheet

ScenarioEnergyApprox. CostNotes
Standard USDT transfer ≈ 65,000 ~1.6 TRX (1.5 energy + ~0.1 bandwidth) Most wallets/contracts fall here.
First transfer to inactive / empty address ≈ 131,000 ~3.1 TRX (≈3.02 energy + ~0.1 bandwidth) Allowance init / account activation can double energy once.

Keep each transfer ≈ 1.6 TRX

  1. Rent energy before sending (or use the per-transaction plan).
  2. Keep a small TRX buffer for bandwidth (or freeze a bit of TRX).
  3. Avoid sending to brand-new addresses when possible.
